## Common Sections (all types)

### Gap-to-Research Mapping Table

```
| Research Gap | Proposed Research Content | Innovation Point | Expected Outcome |
|-------------|--------------------------|-----------------|------------------|
| Gap 1       | Content 1                | Innovation 1    | Outcome 1        |

```

### References Format Quick Reference

**GB/T 7714** (Chinese):
```
[1] Author1, Author2. Title[J]. Journal Name, Year, Volume(Issue): Pages.
```

**APA 7th**:
```
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title. Journal Name, Volume(Issue), Pages. DOI
```

**IEEE**:
```
A. A. Author and B. B. Author, "Title," Journal Name, vol. X, no. Y, pp. Z, Year, doi: XXX.
```
